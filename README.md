# Jazsam

Jazsam is a web-based e-commerce coffee store located in **Legazpi, Albay**, built to make ordering drinks fast, easy, and convenient online. The platform allows customers to browse and order a variety of **milktea, coffee, and other beverages** from anywhere.

## Overview

The Jazsam website is designed for customers who want a simple and accessible way to order their favorite drinks online. It provides an easy-to-use interface for viewing products, selecting beverages, and placing orders through a web-based system.

## Features

* Online ordering for milktea, coffee, and other beverages
* User-friendly web interface
* Product browsing by beverage category
* Convenient ordering process for customers
* Designed for local accessibility in Legazpi, Albay

## Purpose

The main goal of Jazsam is to provide a convenient digital ordering experience for beverage lovers in Legazpi, Albay. By bringing the store online, customers can save time and enjoy a smoother ordering process without needing to visit the shop physically.

## Target Users

Jazsam is intended for:

* Customers looking to order drinks online
* Coffee and milktea lovers in Legazpi, Albay
* Users who prefer a fast and convenient web-based ordering system

## Product Offerings

Jazsam offers a selection of beverages, including:

* Milktea
* Coffee
* Other refreshing beverages

## Technologies

This project is a **web-based e-commerce system** developed to support online ordering and product management.

## Vision

Jazsam aims to become a reliable and convenient local online beverage ordering platform that connects customers in Legazpi, Albay with quality drinks and a smooth digital shopping experience.

## Getting Started

To get started with Jazsam:

1. Open the web application in your browser.
2. Browse the available beverage categories.
3. Select your desired drinks.
4. Place your order online.
